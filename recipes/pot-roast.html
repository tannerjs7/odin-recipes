Pot Roast

Description

A pot-roast, is an old-fashioned method of cooking a solid piece of meat with a little water in a pot on top of the stove. An occasional pot roast of the top of the round, or a roast of lamb, or a piece of corned beef can be used.

Ingredients

  • Chuck Roast
  • Potatoes
  • Carrots
  • Beef Broth

Steps

  1. Sear beef
  2. Add broth
  3. Add veggies
  4. Serve
